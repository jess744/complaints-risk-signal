RAG Framework
Green = current month <10% above prior Amber = 10–25% above prior Red = >25% above prior
Comparison method: Current month actual vs prior month actual. Weekly task runs each Monday — current month shown as projected until month closes.
Overall RAG: Green = all Green. Amber = any Amber or 1 Red. Red = 2+ Reds or 3+ Ambers.

Category definitions:
· Regulatory & Legal: Source = BBB, BCA, ACCC, ATIA. Absolute count. Green = 0, Amber = 1+.
· Reputational: Two signals tracked separately. (1) Public Trustpilot score — the all-time cumulative rating visible to customers; target AU >4.8, US >4.7; Red if 0.2 below target. (2) Weekly review quality — avg score of new reviews received that week only; does not affect the public score but is an early operational signal; Amber = any week below target in past 4 weeks, Red = 2 consecutive weeks 0.2 below target. The monthly risk register shows public score as the primary signal; weekly review quality detail lives in the weekly ops view.
· Policy & Product: Complaint Theme = "Policy Issue - NEW" or "Product Issue - NEW". Absolute count vs prior month. All cases included — discrete events flagged in commentary.
· Service Errors: Service Handling is not "Properly Handled", not "NA", and not blank — must have an explicit failure reason. Absolute count vs prior month.
· Dispute / Chargeback Risk: Indication of Possible Dispute = Yes. Absolute count vs prior month. All cases included — NK contribution flagged separately.
· Discrete events: External events (airline insolvency etc.) included in totals but flagged in commentary. Excluded from baseline reset — recommend setting new baseline once cohort resolves.
