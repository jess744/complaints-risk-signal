Comparison method: Final months compared directly (no projection). Monthly run on 5th of each month using prior month final data.
Overall RAG: Green = all Green. Amber = any Amber or 1 Red. Red = 2+ Reds or 3+ Ambers. May 2026 = Red (3 Reds: Total Escalations, Dispute Risk, Policy & Product — all event-driven by NK/Spirit cluster).

Category definitions:
· Regulatory & Legal: Source = BBB, BCA, ACCC, ATIA. Absolute count. Green = 0, Amber = 1+, Red = 2+.
· Reputational: Two signals. (1) Public Trustpilot score — all-time cumulative rating; target AU >4.8, US >4.7. (2) Weekly review quality — avg score of new reviews received that week; Amber = any week below target in past 4 weeks, Red = 2 consecutive weeks 0.2 below target (AU <4.6, US <4.5). Public scores fetched via Chrome on monthly run.
· Policy & Product: Complaint Theme = "Policy Issue - NEW" or "Product Issue - NEW". Absolute count vs NK-excluded prior month standard. NK cases flagged in cluster section.
· Service Errors: Service Handling is not "Properly Handled", not "NA", not blank — must have explicit failure reason. Absolute count vs prior month actual.
· Dispute / Chargeback Risk: Indication of Possible Dispute = Yes. Absolute count vs NK-excluded prior month standard. NK cases tracked separately in cluster section.
· Discrete events: External events (airline insolvency etc.) flagged in cluster section. NK cases excluded from standard baselines for RAG comparison purposes.
